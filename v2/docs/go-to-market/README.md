## Overview

OpenVibe's GTM strategy is built on three layers:

1. **Movement** (vibeorg.com) - Thought leadership, community, partner recruitment
2. **Open Source** (open-vibe.org, GitHub) - Technical credibility, developer ecosystem
3. **Commercial** (vibe.us) - Product sales, customer acquisition

## Key Decisions

### Brand Names & Domains

| Asset | Name/Domain | Purpose |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Movement** | vibeorg.com | Thought leadership, "Vibe your organization" |
| **Open Source** | open-vibe.org | Technical docs, developer community |
| **Product** | vibe.us | Commercial product, sales |
| **GitHub Org** | openvibeorg | Open source repositories |

### Taglines

- **Vision**: "Vibe your organization"
- **Product**: "The workspace where humans and agents work together"
- **Technical**: "Open infrastructure for human+agent collaboration"

### Positioning Hierarchy

```
Level 3: Movement
  "Vibe your organization" - Transform how organizations work
    ↓
Level 2: Infrastructure
  "Open platform for human+agent collaboration" - Technical foundation
    ↓
Level 1: Product
  "Vibe AI Workspace" - Commercial product with features
```

### Differentiation (4 Pillars)

1. **Open Source** - Transparent, auditable, ownable
2. **Multi-Model** - Claude, GPT, Gemini, or your own
3. **Flexible Deployment** - Cloud, on-premise, hybrid, edge
4. **Partner Ecosystem** - Distributed through trusted advisors
